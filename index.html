About me

I build things on the internet and think a lot about how technology shapes people.

I'm a second-year Computer Science student with a love for clean code and even cleaner design. When I'm not knee-deep in a side project, you'll find me outdoors or buried in a good book. I believe the best software is invisible — it just works, and it works beautifully.

Originally from Bhubaneswar, India, I grew up tinkering with electronics before discovering that software could let me build anything without needing a soldering iron.

"The best way to predict the future is to build it."
